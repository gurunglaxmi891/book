Book that every college student should read


5 amazing and impactful books for teenagers


Hey Teenagers! Let’s talk about reading novels, the ultimate adventure you can embark on without leaving your cozy spot. Forget about the mundane and dive into the the outrageous, hilarious and heartwarming tales that authors have concocted just for you. Need a break from from reality? Books have got your back!


Here are 5 must read books that every college students must read at least once.


Everything is F*cked(A book about Hope)


It is a self-help book by Mark Manson that explores the concept of Hope and its role in our lives. Our modern world is full of distractions and that we often fill our lives with the meaningless activities to distract ourselves from the bigger questions about life. Hope is essential to our well-being and happiness, but we often place our hope in the wrong things.


This is How you Heal


A Brianna West’s book, a collection of short pieces on what it means to heal and how you can go about doing that in small ways. This is one of those books that I think everybody can relate to regardless of what’s going on in your life or what stage of life you are in. This book is like a best friend, it gives you the best advice when you are ready to listen and pick it up.


The Mountain is you


A book by Brianna Wiest that aims to help readers overcome their fears and insecurities. It helps you to face your emotions and thoughts. Not a life changing book but a must read if you are interested in personal growth or inner child healing.


The Strength in our Scars


A simply amazing book written by Bianca Sparacino . If you are going through a breakup, confusing love life or drastic change, this is definitely the book for you and also recommended it to anyone with mental struggles. The words really help you open your eyes and mind to what you are capable of, and how you can be the best version of yourself you can be.


Mindset : The New Psychology of Success


A self-help book by Carol S.Dwerk that explores the concept of mindset and its impact on our lives. The book tells that our mindset, or the way we think about ourselves and our abilities, can have a significant impact on our success and happiness.
